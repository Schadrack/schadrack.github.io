CELEBRATIONS

This word CELEBRATIONS can be used as an accronym which explains the important things to take care if people need to be healthy. These are like instructions to follow if we need to grow well, having strengthes and ability to fight against diseases. These tips are indispensable not only to children who still grow but also to adult people and espeecially, to old people who still need to keep life healthy, as they are in their senile periods. Here we can find the meaning of each letter in this word. And the idea behind Celebrations is to have a life fully of happiness, free of diseases and impact positively to the life of other creatures.
